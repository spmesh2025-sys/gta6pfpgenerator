About this tool

GTAVI PFP Generator is a free, fan-made browser tool that lets you drop your logo onto Vice City-inspired backgrounds and export a polished profile picture in seconds.

100% private — everything runs locally in your browser. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

Quick Workflow

  1. Pick a background from the grid or upload your own.
  2. Upload your subject/logo — PNG ideal, photos work too.
  3. Remove background — Solid for flat-colour logos; AI for photos & complex images.
  4. Resize & position with sliders. Hit ⊕ Centre to snap to middle.
  5. Erase any leftover bits using the Eraser tool. Ctrl+Z to undo.
  6. Export as PNG or JPEG at full 1080×1080 px.

Pro Tip

After AI removal, use the Eraser at a small brush size to clean up any halo or fringe around edges for a sharp result.
